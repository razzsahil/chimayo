Our Story

Five decades of fire

Founded by the Trujillo family in 1972, Chimayó has spent five decades translating the smoke, chile, and corn of northern New Mexico into a modern stone-fired kitchen. We mill our masa, smoke our chiles, and source from regenerative ranches within 200 miles of Durango.
